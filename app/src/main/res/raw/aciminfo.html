INSTRUCTIONS

Thank you for purchasing this app. The design is meant to feel relaxed and peaceful, yet give you ready, easy access to the main study material of ACIM, namely the Text and Workbook. Even though you are likely to find the layout fairly straight forward, here are some guidelines that might help you.

The 'LESSONS OF LOVE' Screen

The Workbook for Students is the main part of the actual mind training of A Course in Miracles, essential to the goal of the healing curriculum, and foremost in the 365 lessons of love exercises that are accessed from this screen.

1) 'TODAY'S LESSON' - Here set your LESSON FOR THE DAY in the numerical input box and then tap OK to bring up the lesson. By default it is set at lesson 1 to begin.

NOTE: This box is always viable and can be easily changed if you need. However if you set it and forget it, each day will automatically advance from the number set to the next lesson at midnight provided you have done your lesson for that day. Otherwise the lesson is waiting for you to do it. Simply click OK whenever you want to go to your lesson.

2) 'SELECT LESSON' - Use this box to select SPECIFIC LESSON. At times you might want to read a particular lesson without upsetting your daily lesson. Just type in a value between 1 and 365 and press the GO button to bring up that lesson.

3) SCREEN SCROLLS - up and down smoothly for each lesson if it is needed.

4) TO ACCESS OTHER LESSONS - just flick sideways, to quickly access lessons either forward or back in sequence .

NOTE: Take care when viewing a lesson - don't flick when scrolling up and down. Instead slide with your finger gently pressed against the screen, or make your slide longer than just a flick. This will ensure you stay on the lesson you are reading.

5) TO ZOOM IN AND ZOOM OUT - use the +/- button that will appear in the bottom right corner when you slide or touch the screen slightly. Using this button will ensure the best text layout on the screen. You can also use two fingers in a pinching manner for bigger or smaller text but this might mean you lose your place.

A little experimentation with all these controls should give you a pleasing result.

The 'RETURN TO LOVE' Screen

The Text of A Course in Miracles explains the time/space situation, special relationships and misunderstandings we are faced with in our conditioned world experience we call life, and then gives directives as to our best path of response to find ultimate salvation and true home in Love. Students often elect to study this concurrently as they do the lessons. There's no set approach for the text. It's up to you. This loving guidance is accessed from this screen.

1) SELECT CHAPTER - Tap this horizontal bar to open up a dropdown screen with 31 Chapters listed. Scroll through this list and tap on the chapter you choose to select it. A second horizontal bar will appear.

NOTE: Buttons highlight when tapped to indicate they are activated.

2) SELECT SECTION - Tap this second horizontal bar to open a dropdown screen with the sections for the current chapter listed. Scroll the list and tap the section you want to select it.

3) CHAPTER AND SECTION - are now displayed together on the two bars for your review. Change them if you like or proceed to the text by touching the GO button to bring up your selection.

NOTE: Any section in a chapter can access the rest of that chapter by scrolling up or down. But to access another chapter you need to use the back button on your phone and reselect from the 'RETURN TO LOVE' screen. Also note that if your phone is set for rotation, landscape or portrait orientation is supported for both Text and Lessons.
