Lesson 120

For morning and evening review:

(109) I rest in God.

I rest in God today, and let Him work in me and through me, while I rest in Him in quiet and in perfect certainty.

(110) I am as God created me.

I am God's Son.
Today I lay aside all sick illusions of myself, and let my Father tell me Who I really am.

On the hour:

I rest in God.

On the half hour:

I am as God created me.
