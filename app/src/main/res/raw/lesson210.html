Lesson 210

I am not a body. I am free.
For I am still as God created me.

(190) I choose the joy of God instead of pain.

Pain is my own idea.
It is not a Thought of God, but one I thought apart from Him and from His Will.
His Will is joy, and only joy for His beloved Son.
And that I choose, instead of what I made.

I am not a body. I am free.
For I am still as God created me.
