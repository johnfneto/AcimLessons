Lesson 220

I am not a body. I am free,
For I am still as God created me.

(200) There is no peace except the peace of God.

Let me not wander from the way of peace, for I am lost on other roads than this.
But let me follow Him Who leads me home, and peace is certain as the Love of God.

I am not a body. I am free.
For I am still as God created me.
